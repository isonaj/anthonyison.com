I have been skirting around the outer edges of some meetups, interested in giving a talk, but not sure what to talk about. I have recently been given some great advice: "There is someone who wants to hear what you have to say. They are the ones who will come to hear you speak."

> There is someone who wants to hear what you have to say. They are the ones who will come to hear you speak. - Jason Taylor
